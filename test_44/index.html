Retro Futuristic Car

Project Goals:
• to combine retro car with future technologies;
• to provide a flexible asset that can be used in web design, digital illustrations, educational tools, and interactive media.

Retro Futuristic Car | 3D version

This project showcases a digitally rendered, futuristic car model designed for an animation or video game. The car has a sleek, angular design with a modern, almost retro-futuristic aesthetic. The body of the car is predominantly composed of geometric shapes, giving it a stylized, blocky appearance reminiscent of early 3D video game graphics but with a contemporary twist.

COVID-19 Space Invaders Game

This project is a creative and educational adaptation of the classic arcade game "Space Invaders," reimagined to raise awareness about COVID-19.

COVID-19 Space Invaders Game | Screenshot

This project aims to combine entertainment with education, highlighting the importance of fighting against COVID-19 in a format that is engaging and accessible to a broad audience. Through this interactive experience, players can learn more about the virus and the collective effort needed to overcome the pandemic.
